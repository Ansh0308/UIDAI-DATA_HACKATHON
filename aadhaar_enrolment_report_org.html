With a median of just 2 enrolments per record and mean of 5.4, the system processes primarily individual or small family units. This indicates:

Business Impact: Efficiency improvements could focus on increasing batch sizes through targeted awareness campaigns.

The daily time series shows consistent baseline activity punctuated by periodic spikes, suggesting:
